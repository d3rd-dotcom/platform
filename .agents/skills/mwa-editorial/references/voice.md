# Voice & Tone

Source of truth: `EDITORIAL.md` (Brand Book v4.0, synced with the Brand Guidelines deck V2). Everything here restates that file. When they disagree, `EDITORIAL.md` wins.

## We sound like

- **Academic, visionary, and intellectually curious** — real substance, no filler.
- **Internet-aware, never trend-hopping** — fluent in how people talk online, without lingo that expires.
- **Calmly skeptical of AI and its hype** — we build with AI and still question it in the open. Evidence first.
- **Awe and intellectual rebellion, delivered calmly** — big questions about hidden structures, without urgency-bait.
- **Positive, encouraging, and clear** — the lift comes from concrete wins, not hype adjectives.
- **Statistics, theory, and academic framing** — we investigate hidden structures and show the work.
- **Cyberpunk philosophy and decentralization, kept legible.**

## We do NOT sound like

| Anti-pattern | Example | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Corporate wellness | "Optimize your life!" | Name the actual mechanism or the concrete win |
| Vague self-help | "Step into your best era" | Replace with the specific behavioral frame |
| A recruiter | "Invite your friends and earn!" | Cut referral-bait; talk about the work, not the funnel |

## The test before posting (run it first)

> **Does this communicate an educational gameworld, an academic angle, or a feature that actually matters?**

If it doesn't land on one of those three concretely, cut it or ground it.

## What we are (30-second version — memorize)

> **Mental Wealth Academy is a social-first gameworld for learning, research, and rewards.** A gameworld built around behavioral psychology, custom science tools, and Blue, our AI companion, on a blockchain foundation with permanent rewards, ownership, and anonymous-by-default participation.

- **The product:** a co-op, interactive, anonymous-by-default gameworld that feels intellectually refreshing.
- **The innovation:** we blend AI, blockchain, and science with gaming in a way that supports education rather than just wrapping a token around a product.
- **The difference:** social-first. Courses, missions, quests, and tools create micro-learning moments with real rewards, without turning people into targets or leads.

## Core pillars

1. **Education** — accelerate quality education: sensible, free to all, and rewarding to learn.
2. **Environment** — mental wealth is our social ability to redefine prosperity beyond material assets.
3. **Evolution** — invest in human potential; compounding virtuous actions lead to a better future.

Objective / tagline: *"Investing in the future of humanity, with the heart of tomorrow."* Grounded meaning: small, consistent actions today make the future measurably better.

## Who we serve (pick the reader before you write)

| Audience | Who | What they want |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | Gen-Z individuals seeking structured growth, **ages 18 and up** | Grew up online; drawn to psychology and self-improvement; motivated by XP, rewards, and badges; want accountability, real stakes, and a communal social environment; thrive on quick, well-scoped tasks |
| **Secondary** | Scientists and academics — including citizen scientists, status seekers, and special-interest groups | Collaborative testing and experimentation; research tied to live participants and real behavioral data; an AI companion that generates novel datasets |

Most consumer surfaces (homepage, app onboarding) speak to the Primary reader. Don't mix the two audiences inside one section.

## Blue in one line

Blue is an RAG model AI agent who reviews MWA archives and treasury activity. To users she is the mascot of Mental Wealth Academy and a champion for your mental wealth. For writing AS her, use `mwa-blue`.

## Positioning (internal — keeps us honest)

We are a research cohort for personal development, using agentic smart contracts and a living educational system. Governance means how groups make decisions together; Blue's autonomous judgment is part of the governance layer, and quest completion is part of the self-repair loop.

In outward copy, state what we are and let it stand — never define us by contrast.
