# Part 5 — Feedback Loop and Metrics

Source: [Email Marketing Architecture](https://app.notion.com/p/3a497ad572998101bd68c6d12bdd70c8) (Notion)

**Measure per email:**

- Open rate (subject and preview working).
- Click / CTA rate (the one job landed).
- Mission or step completion after the click (the real goal, not just the click).
- Return within the next window (did the streak continue).
- Unsubscribe and spam rate (frequency and relevance staying healthy).

**Then:**

- Feed winners and losers back into the brand-filter (step 5) and pattern library (step 4).
- Prune non-engagers on a schedule to protect deliverability.
- Keep two subject-line variants per send and let the slice pick the winner.
