## Verdict standard

- **Verified** — the post is publishable from the cited evidence, subject to its existing production-day guardrail.
- **Qualified** — the core idea is supported, but the wording or source field needs the exact correction below.
- **Blocked** — a participant record, consent record, production state, first-party measurement, or other named proof artifact is still missing. Do not publish until the condition is satisfied.

The audit checked memos 01–09, the four archived source files and their hashes, primary publisher pages, and the current repository implementation. The archived hashes match `09-source-pack.md`. ## Proof-field audit

The draft’s evidence fields fall into three groups:

1. **Directly supporting:** days 1–5, 8–11, 13, 17, 20–23, and 27–28 cite real memos, primary studies, or repository files relevant to the claim.
2. **Supporting after repair or tighter wording:** day 7 has a broken local filename; days 4, 10, 21, 23, 26, and 28 overstate or misdescribe what the cited implementation or study proves.
3. **Conditions rather than evidence:** days 6, 12, 15, 16, 18, 19, 24, 25, 29, and 30 name artifacts that still need to be created, selected, audited, or consented. These fields cannot substantiate a caption written in the past tense today.
