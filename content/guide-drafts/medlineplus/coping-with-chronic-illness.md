A long-term, or chronic, illness can disrupt your life in many ways. You may be
tired and in pain, and it might affect your appearance, physical abilities, and
independence. Not being able to work can create financial strain too.

**How it can affect you**

These changes can bring on stress, anxiety, and anger. If they do, it's worth
seeking support. A trained counselor can help you build strategies to regain a
sense of control, and support groups can help you see you're not alone and pick
up new tips for coping.

**Learning about your illness**

You may be able to manage your illness better by learning more about it. Check
that the information you find is reliable, and look for a health care provider
you can trust.
